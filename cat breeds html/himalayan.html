Himalayan

Himalayan

Features

  • Long Limbs
  • Brown, Short Coats
  • Muscular

Character Trait

  • Curious
  • Affectionate
  • Active

Origin

  • Asia
  • Thai-Burma

The Himalayan is (not surprisingly) a blend of Persian and Siamese cats. They were bred to have both their straits: the Persian’s long fur and scrunched-up face, and the Siamese’s coat pattern and personality. With such a close-relationship to the Persian, these cats need the same level of care and maintenance. Their long fur coat may be beautiful, but due to its length, it can be a lot of work with frequent baths and brushing that the cat can’t perform for itself. However, just like the Persian, the Himalayan makes a wonderful lap cat for a loving owner. What makes them ever better is that with the personality of a Siamese, they can be as affectionate and sweet! They love spending time with their owners, enjoying comfy spots, and LOVES to be cuddled and pet. Overall, if you love the look of the Persian lap-cat, but want the coat and personality of a Siamese, a Himalayan may be perfect for you!
