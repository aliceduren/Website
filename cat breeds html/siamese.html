Siamese

Siamese

Features

  • Brown and White Coat
  • Slender Body
  • Blue Crossed-Eyes

Character Traits

  • Extroverted
  • Chatty
  • Friendly

Origin

  • Southeast Asia
  • Thailand

The Siamese cat is considered the most “extroverted” breed of the domestic cat kingdom. They have very distinct traits, such as their sleek bodies, as well as their brown and tan coat, with brown fur extending to the face and extremities (arms and legs). They may also have very blue (and very crossed) eyes due to their controlled breeding habits. Like the Bengal cat, they have associations with Asia, specifically Thailand as their original home. Later on, they became a common European house pet, and still remain to this day! The most distinct trait Siamese cats have (aside from their eyes and coats) are their wonderfully playful personalities! They always love to play, cuddle, chase things around, and show their affection for their favorite human and other cat friends. For this reason, they are quite a popular house cat. Just like the Bengal, they also have A LOT to say, so if you’re up for a daily conversation, a Siamese cat might be for you!
