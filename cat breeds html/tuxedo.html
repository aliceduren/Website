Tuxedo

Tuxedo

Features

  • Black and White Coat
  • White Paws
  • Green Eyes

Character Traits

  • Friendly
  • Shy
  • Loving

Origin

  • U.S.A.

Though the Tuxedo is not exactly a distinct “breed,” their coat pattern is what truly defines this cat. Coming from Egypt, they have had a distinct coloration of black and white in various places. This usually forms a pattern of black around the top of the head, body and legs, and white at the bottom of the face, belly, and paws, making it look as if they’re wearing a literal tuxedo. The thickness of the coat tends to be quite long, but again, it varies from breed to breed. Though the personality varies from tuxedos, they can be seen both as outdoor and indoor cats. They may be expected to have more introverted traits, but as they warm up to their owner, they playfulness can start to show, and their personalities begin to come out more! If you ever see a “Tuxie” out and about, know that they are one distinguished cat and could make a loving house pet!
