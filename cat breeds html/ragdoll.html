Ragdoll

Ragdoll

Feautres

  • Brown and White Coat
  • Long, Fluffy Fur
  • Round Face

Character Trait

  • Loving
  • Shy
  • Lazy

Origin

  • U.S.A.

With the loving personality of the Siamese, and the low-maintenance of the British Shorthair, the Ragdoll is a fan favorite for any cat lover! They have a naturally soft disposition and can be loving in all the right ways! They technically come from the Angora cat breed, but don’t have a specific point of origin, as they were humanly bred in a controlled environment. They have a very distinct coat with their main colors being white/cream and brown: white forming the whole body, and brown being around the eyes, ears, and some random spots on the body. They are also very long-haired, so their shedding may be a chore to deal with. Overall, Ragdolls make wonderful house cats for a reason: they were bred to be house cats! They may be shy at first, but they making very loving pets, and may flop down onto a keyboard or two. They are very docile and mellow cats that show their affection often. If you’re looking for a cat that doesn’t need too much action, but still has a loving and sweet personality, a ragdoll may just be what you’re looking for! (These cats also go well in pairs, so adopting a sibling of theirs may also be a good move for social reasons.)
