British Shorthair

British Shorthair

Feautres

  • Solid-Colored Fur
  • Round Face
  • Short Body

Character Trait

  • Relaxed
  • Self-Sustaining
  • Aloof

Origin

  • Great Britan
  • England

The British Shorthair is considered one of the most easy-going and low-maintenance cat breeds for a home. The origin of this cat is in the name: they are one of the oldest breeds to come from Great Britan and England. Overtime, they were brought over to America to continue their life has wonderful house pets. Though they range in eye color and facial structure, British shorthairs usually have a solid-colored grey coat, as well as a short and round body. As far as personalities, this breed may not be the most affectionate. Like most cats, they prefer to be alone and receive mild pets and sparse attention. However, that doesn’t mean they don’t enjoy being around their owners! Though they may not be the best lap cats like the Persian, if their trust is gained, they can make a relaxing companion to be around. If a secure connection is made, they’ll love you all the more for understanding their introverted and relaxed personalities!
