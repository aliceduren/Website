Reach Out and Help Today!

Flatbush Cats

This is a nonprofit organization currently working in Brooklyn to a build veterinary clinic for cat owners with low incomes, as well as TNR volunteers. They are currently working to get as many cats as possible spayed, neutuered, and put in a safe environment to fostered, and hopefully adopted. They're always teaching about how to foster correctly and what amazing things can happen when a properly socialized kitten is sent to a loving forever home. They do amazing work and they could always use your help and resources!

YouTube and Website

The Cat House on the Kings

This is a nonprofit organization that is a no-cage, no-kill, lifetime cat sanctuary and adoption center. Located in California, they work to put cats in a safe environment that allows them to be adopted and placed in a loving home. They also work to prevent overpopulation through TNR services. They frequently teach about safe care for cats, and work hard in what they do!

Website

Best Friends Animal Society

The Best Friends Animal Society has worked to build a no-kill animal sanctuary since 1984. Through their efforts, they've been able to build lifesaving centers all over the world, and always make sure to provide the best place for their cats (and various animals). Consider donating to their cause and help them provide more shelters for animals everywhere!

Website

Alley Cat Allies

Alley Cat Allies is a wonderful resource for leadership on how to best provide for cats! They work with many shelters all over America to educate people on cat care and how to give a feline the best life they can have. They've created multiple campaigns, and could always use your help. Donate and become a fellow Cat Ally!
