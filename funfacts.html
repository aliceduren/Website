Some Fun Cat Facts!

Royal Origins

Cats were originally considered to be Royalty in ancient Egypt

Healing Purr

A cat’s purr has self-healing properties. Low-frequency vibrations can help with bone-marrow, and cats may purr to both heal themselves and other people.

Cat Breeds

There are 40-70 different cat breeds in the world.

Born Blind

All cats are born blind and it may take up to 2 weeks at most for their eyes to open up! (After this, all kittens have blue eyes after opening them, and can take several weeks for their natural eye color to show up.)

Jumpers

Cats can jump 5-6 times their own height!

Big Sleepers

Cats sleep for around 13 to 16 hours a day (70% of their life). This isn’t just special to domestic cats: big cats such as lions and tigers also sleep for a long time.

1 to 5

1 year of a cat’s life equals to 5 years of a humans live. (This would make my 15-year old cat, 75 years old!)

Still a Kitten

Sometimes, if a cat is separated too early from its mother and siblings, they will continue to replicate behaviors they did as a kitten, such as kneading and suckling on various surfaces. This is a way of comforting themselves.

I Bring You Gifts!

Since cats will reside in colonies, they will occasionally bring each other gifts, such as dead (or alive) birds, rodents, flowers, and other items they think are useful. If a cat ever brings you such gifts, just know they see you as a fellow feline friend and want to help you out.
